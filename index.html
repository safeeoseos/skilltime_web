Now this era belongs to a sharing era. We hereby plan to create a skill service trading platform, which can sell personal skills, so that users with skills can do part-time skills, realize personal skills and obtain income. We can publish and sell our own skills online. The demander can find qualified skilled talents through the platform, complete skill transactions and obtain income value for skills. Users can also publish skill needs online, Skilled talents receive orders online and complete transactions according to their own conditions, so as to maximize the value of their personal skills.Users with certain personal time and skills can participate.As long as you have skills, whether it's taking photos, programming, doing handicrafts, teaching people to do PPT, or playing games, you can hang it up as long as you're good at it. The price is set by yourself, and you'll buy it if the demand side needs it. Of course, the demand side can also hang up their own needs and let skilled talents choose to receive orders. All core logics are controlled by EOS smart contract to ensure the rights and interests of both parties. After the demand initiated by the demander is received by skilled talents, the demander needs to mortgage the amount of EOS negotiated by both parties and the amount of EOS to be compensated by the receiving party who may not complete the task. Why should both supply and demand need collateral? This is to ensure the interests of both sides.
